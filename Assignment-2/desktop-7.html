Pushkar
Home to rare temples, shrines and picturesque locations, Pushkar in Rajasthan is where tourists throng to find their peace, and lose themselves in the rare beauty of Mother Nature.

Brahma Temple
Brahma, the creator of universe, is a revered god in this temple in Pushkar. The temple is famous for attracting lakhs of pilgrims every year.

Merta City
Merta is an ancient city near Pushkar, known for witnessing many historic battles in the past. This is also the birthplace of famous poetess and Krishna’s devotee, Meerabai.

Rangji Temple
One of the oldest temples in Pushkar, Rangji temple is dedicated to Lord Rangji—an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. The architecture here is an amalgamation of Dravidian, Mughal and Rajputana styles.

Savitri Temple
Tucked on the Ratnagiri Hill, Savitri Temple attracts devotees with its interesting folklore. The trek to the top of Ratnagiri Hill could be tiresome, but it is believed to wash away the sins of devotees.

Pushkar Lake
Located in the Ajmer district of Rajasthan, Pushkar Lake has a beautiful mythological story attached to it. Thousands of pilgrimage throng here for the famous Pushkar Fair.

Apteshwar Temple
Dedicated to Lord Shiva, this place too has a lot of significance for its mythological roots. Dating back to the 12th century, it is believed that this temple was built by Lord Brahma.
